About

I’m Kaipo — an artist from Hawai‘i, living between Las Vegas and California.

I make music, paint, draw, and sculpt — building worlds by hand and sound. Every song starts in Logic, recorded from my closet.

Lately, I’ve been merging my art with AI to create cinematic videos — a fusion of handmade and digital imagination.
