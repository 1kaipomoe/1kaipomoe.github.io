About

I grew up surrounded by the stillness of the Hawaiian islands, then found myself in the neon heat of Las Vegas. My music lives in that in-between — where the warmth of Hawai‘i drifts into the electric pulse of West Coast pop.

From the start, my creativity was restless. I sketched my heroes, sculpted small beings, stitched fabric into form, and painted stories onto blank surfaces. Each medium was an abstract doorway. Music and film arrived later, adding to the chorus.

Now I make songs that blur the handmade with the digital, where melody becomes atmosphere and images unfold into worlds.
